Recent Projects

I wanted to learn to use xml, SQL (SQLAlchemy), analyze some not-so-big data and play with some visualization problems.

Mondify

Mondify is a Python program which fakes new versions of Piet Mondrian's painting, "Victory Boogie Woogie".

Snorweg Routeplanner

An app for Dutch railway users with "trajectabonnements" (free travel over a fixed route) to determine the cheapest way to travel outside of their traject.

Fare is Fair

Are some segments of the Dutch railway system more expensive than others? Is it faster to drive or take the train?

Older Work

Research from my PhD on the statistical mechanics of granular materials.

Fluidization and temperature in a liquid made of glass beads

A container of glass beads behaves as a liquid when the beads are excited by rotating a disk in the bottom of the vessel. But how similar is this "granular liquid" to a real liquid? Can you measure the "temperature" of the glass beads?

Statistical mechanics with air hockey pucks

Do 150 air hockey pucks behave like air molecules?
